Description

The 3D Building Visualization Plugin transforms how you interact with architectural and urban planning data in Re:Earth. Create stunning three-dimensional representations of buildings, infrastructure, and urban environments with advanced rendering capabilities and interactive features.

Perfect for architects, urban planners, real estate developers, and city officials who need to visualize complex building data in an intuitive and compelling way. The plugin supports multiple data formats and provides extensive customization options for materials, lighting, and environmental effects.

Key Features

  • Advanced 3D Rendering: High-quality building visualization with realistic materials, shadows, and lighting effects
  • Multi-format Support: Import from CAD, BIM, GeoJSON, and other popular architectural data formats
  • Interactive Navigation: Smooth camera controls with fly-through capabilities and predefined viewpoints
  • Height Extrusion: Automatically generate 3D buildings from 2D footprints with height data
  • Material Library: Extensive collection of realistic building materials and textures
  • Performance Optimized: Efficient rendering for large-scale urban environments with thousands of buildings
  • Team Collaboration: Share 3D scenes and collaborate on urban planning projects
